    Walkability in Singapore (IS Project Experience - Industry Research)

    An industry research project with Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA) as sponsor client for the SMU module, IS485 IS Project Experience (Research) to create a walkability index calculation model to identify walkability gaps for elderly and age-friendly long term planning. Project served as a capstone project of the degree programme.

    Tools: ArcGIS Pro, Python Programming

    Google Discover and the Echo-Chamber Effect

    A team project for the SMU module, IS457 Fairness in Socio-technical Systems to audit the algorithm behind Google Discover and understand whether or not Google Discover is proliferating content based on users' interests and hence enforcing confirmation bias which results in echo-chamber effect.

    Tools: Google Discover, Google Takeout, Python Programming, Microsoft Excel, Google Spreadsheet, Google Drive, YouTube

    Perception of Vaping Among University Students

    A team project for the SMU module, SMT202 Analytics Applications for Smart Living in collaboration with Health Promotion Board to understand how social environment in university affects students’ attitude, behaviour, and perception of vaping.

    Tools: Python Programming, Data Analytics, Machine Learning
